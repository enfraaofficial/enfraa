Frequently Answered Queries

Q1. Is this a co-working space?

No, Enfraa is not a co-working space. It is a SaaS platform providing software engines to help local businesses manage billing, inventories, and cashbooks.

Q2. Do we need to pay to use the space/software?

Enfraa offers a complete 30-day trial plan for only ₹99. Our monthly and annual enterprise options scale dynamically with your business needs.

Q3. How do you sustain?

Enfraa is supported by our monthly and annual merchant subscription model and backing partners, allowing us to keep developing local tech tools.
